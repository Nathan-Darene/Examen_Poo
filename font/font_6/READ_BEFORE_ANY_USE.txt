Whitestone Formal is a script font of vintage elegance.

Its weighty characters, coupled with the script's flowing movements, create a sense of depth and solidity that's both eye-catching and classy. 

The Victorian inspiration is evident in every curve, bringing an air of decorative opulence to any text. Crafted with attention to detail, Whitestone Formal adapts its historical influences to fit contemporary needs, offering a timeless appeal that transcends trends.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers. 

Created by Mans Greback, Whitestone Formal is a blending of historical inspiration and modern design. Mans' work reflects typographic traditions and creative vision.
